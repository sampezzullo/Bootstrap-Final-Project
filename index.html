Welcome to Maximalist Christmas

Step into a world where Christmas isn’t just celebrated—it’s elevated. This is your one-stop inspiration hub for decking the halls in true maximalist fashion. From color-packed trees dripping with ornaments to gift wrapping so elaborate it’s practically art, this site is all about turning up the volume on holiday cheer.
Whether you’re a seasoned maximalist or just ready to ditch minimalist trends for something more festive, you’ll find bold ideas, dazzling décor tips, and endless inspiration to make this season unforgettable.
Because more is more—this season, go big, bold, and beautifully extra!

Celebrate Personality & Individuality

Maximalism lets you express yourself fully. It’s a celebration of what makes you unique and allows you to incorporate your favorite colors, patterns, and treasures into your space. Instead of following strict design rules, maximalism invites you to embrace everything you love, making your environment an authentic reflection of who you are.

A Joyous, Over-the-Top Experience

The holiday season is all about joy, and maximalism amplifies that energy. With layers of décor, vibrant colors, and abundant details, it creates a festive environment that feels alive with celebration. Maximalism brings excitement and wonder to every inch of your space, creating a merry and bright atmosphere that’s as fun as it is festive.

The Art of Layering and Richness

Maximalism is the art of layering textures, colors, and elements to create a deeper, more immersive experience. By mixing materials like velvet, metallics, and glass, you bring complexity and richness to your décor. This layered approach adds visual interest and makes every detail pop, transforming your space into a dynamic and unforgettable holiday setting.

Wrap it Extra

Why settle for plain when you can make your presents the star of the tree with this step-by-step tutorial:

Wrap with BOLD wrapping paper

Bold wrapping paper isn’t just for covering gifts—it’s a way to make every present under your tree part of the festive décor. With vibrant colors, eye-catching patterns, and dramatic designs, bold wrapping paper ensures your gifts stand out and add to the holiday magic. It’s an opportunity to express creativity by pairing striking prints with oversized bows, glittering ribbons, or playful toppers, creating a layered and unique look. Whether it’s metallic finishes, colorful plaids, or whimsical motifs, bold wrapping paper transforms your gift-giving into an art form that embodies the joyous, over-the-top spirit of maximalism.

Pick a FUN ribbon

Choosing a fun ribbon is the perfect way to take your holiday gift wrapping to the next level. Ribbons add texture, color, and personality, turning even simple wrapping paper into a festive masterpiece. Whether it’s a glittering metallic, a bold velvet, or a whimsical patterned ribbon, the right choice makes your gift feel extra special and unique. Plus, fun ribbons are versatile—you can layer them, tie oversized bows, or even curl them for added flair. They’re an easy way to infuse creativity into your wrapping and make your gifts as exciting on the outside as they are on the inside!

Top with a QUIRKY ornament

Topping your gift with a quirky ornament is a playful way to add personality and charm to your wrapping. Not only does it make your present stand out, but it also gives the recipient a keepsake they can use for years to come. Whether it’s a tiny disco ball, a vintage figurine, or a glittering snowflake, quirky ornaments add texture, sparkle, and a sense of whimsy that screams holiday cheer. They’re the perfect finishing touch for maximalist wrapping, transforming your gift into a mini work of art that’s as delightful to look at as it is to unwrap!

Fabulous Decor

The key to a maximalist Christmas is fabulous décor layered with an abundance of cool, vintage Christmassy knick-knacks—because when it comes to maximalism, more is always better! Think retro Santas, shimmering tinsel garlands, glass ornaments in every color, and shelves packed with snow globes, nutcrackers, and festive figurines. These unique pieces not only bring nostalgia but also add depth and personality to your holiday aesthetic. Mixing textures, colors, and eras creates a rich, dynamic atmosphere where every corner of your space feels magical. Maximalism is all about embracing abundance, so let your creativity run wild and make your home a Christmas wonderland!

Over-the-Top Tree Styling

If you’re ready to go all out this Christmas, over-the-top tree styling is the way to embrace maximalist holiday magic! Think larger-than-life ornaments, layers of garland dripping in sparkle, and an explosion of colors that radiate festive cheer. A maximalist Christmas tree isn’t afraid to mix textures, patterns, and even themes—combine oversized baubles, velvet bows, glittering ribbons, and quirky accents like candy canes or miniature figurines. Top it off with a show-stopping tree topper, such as a starburst, a feathered angel, or even a cascade of cascading lights. This bold approach creates a tree that’s not just décor—it’s a centerpiece that tells a story and makes a statement of unrestrained holiday joy.


To achieve a maximalist look, you need to think in layers—each element building on the last. Start with a base of lush, full garlands or ribbons. Then, add ornaments in various sizes, textures, and finishes. Finish with fillers like floral picks, feathers, or sprays to make the tree feel abundant and overflowing with detail.


A maximalist tree thrives on drama, so don’t hold back on statement-making pieces. Choose a showstopping tree topper, like a giant starburst or an extravagant bow. Add oversized ornaments, large candy canes, or whimsical figurines throughout the tree to create focal points that grab attention.


Nothing makes a tree sparkle like the perfect lighting strategy. Use multiple layers of lights—warm white, colored, and twinkling lights—for a multidimensional glow. Wrap the lights deep into the tree and along the branches for depth, then use clip-on LED lights to spotlight standout ornaments.
